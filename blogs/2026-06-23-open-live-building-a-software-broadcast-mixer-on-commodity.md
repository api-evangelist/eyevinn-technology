The broadcast switcher has been one of the last pieces of production infrastructure that resisted software-defined approaches. Dedicated hardware mixers from established vendors work well, but they come with costs that are hard to justify for many productions: proprietary hardware, locked-down upgrade paths, and workflows that assume you have a physical control surface in a physical room. Open Live takes a different approach.
