In this blog post we will walk through how you can quickly setup a continue watching micro service for your video player applications using Open Source Cloud and Redis Cloud . Today a user of a video streaming service expects to be able to pick up where they left off, on any of the episodes they watched and on any of the devices they have. To handle that you would need to develop a backend service with endpoints for the video player and device application to write to and read from.
